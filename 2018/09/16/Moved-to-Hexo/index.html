Moved to Hexo

2018-09-16

#meta

I moved my blog to Hexo and dumped Jekyll. I’m so glad I made this change; Hexo is much more feature-packed, easier to use, and the missing features like post tags and such work.

I might have broken the RSS functionality, but other than that, the older posts should still have the exact same URL and should work seemlessly.

Hope the new blog looks better. I used the Anodyne theme for my blog and I think it looks amazing.

It also comes with Disqus support and I’m looking forward to reading the comments!

Thanks for visiting my blog as always.
